Frequently asked questions about Subscription
Management Software

A subscription management software helps you to manage all the activities involved in a subscriber's lifecycle from signing up to your product or service up to the cancellation. Subscription management platforms like chargify allow you to seamlessly automate pricing experiments, free trial management, recurring billing, dunning management, revenue reporting, and customer email communication. Handling cancellations, upgrades, and refunds is easy when you have the right tool to help you out.

Subscription management systems work closely with payment gateways and accounting tools to cater to the dynamic needs of the subscription services. Subscription softwares collects and stores details of a customer's subscription plan, billing details and also integrates with your payment gateways, accounting and taxation systems to meet the complex requirements of running a subscription business.

Subscriptions have become a huge popularity in the last decade with their predictable revenue model and steady cash flow. Every business, irrespective of their size, location and industry is trying to capitalize this trend. But starting a subscription business comes with its own set of challenges and hardships, like sending recurring invoices every month, managing subscriber's plan and payment related information, dealing with automatic cancellations, dunning and more. Online subscription management and recurring billing software helps subscription businesses to flourish by automating all the critical tasks.
